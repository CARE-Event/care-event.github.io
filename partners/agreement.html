Partnership Agreement

This agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between Project CARE and ___ (“Partner”) on this day, 03 March 2023.

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a partnership between Project CARE and Partner for the purpose of collaborating on the upcoming event.

2. Responsibilities

Partner agrees to provide the following services and/or materials in support of the event:

  1. Collaborating with Project CARE to ensure the successful planning and execution of the event.
  2. Fulfilling any obligations agreed upon in writing by both parties.
  3. Providing necessary resources and materials for their involvement in the event.
  4. Adhering to any deadlines or schedules agreed upon in writing by both parties.
  5. Maintaining open communication with Project CARE to address any issues or concerns that may arise during the course of the event.
  6. Complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and policies related to their participation in the event.

3. Term

This Agreement shall be effective as of the date written above and shall continue until the conclusion of the event, unless earlier terminated by mutual agreement of the parties or by either party for any reason.

4. Compensation

There will be no financial compensation provided to the Partner.

5. Confidentiality

The Partner acknowledges that all information disclosed by Project CARE relating to the event is confidential and shall not be disclosed to any third party.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon written notice to the other party.

7. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which Project CARE is located.

8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

By accepting this Agreement, Partner agrees to be bound by its terms and conditions.
